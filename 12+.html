Career Guidance: After 12th Standard

Explore common streams, courses, and entrance exams in India.

Choose Your 12th Standard Stream:

Completed 12th Standard
Science (PCM - Physics, Chemistry, Maths) +
Engineering (B.E. / B.Tech) +
Key Exams:
  • JEE (Main & Advanced) +

    • Eligibility (JEE Main): Passed 12th (or equivalent) with Physics & Maths as compulsory subjects, plus one from Chem/Bio/Biotech/Tech.Voc. No age limit. Appears in year of passing 12th + next 2 years.

    Eligibility (JEE Advanced): Must qualify JEE Main (top ~2.5 Lakh candidates) & meet 12th % criteria (e.g., 75% aggregate or top 20 percentile - varies, check official site) or be in final year of 12th.

    JEE Main (NTA) | JEE Advanced

  • State CETs (e.g., MHT-CET, KCET, EAMCET) +

    • Eligibility: Varies by state. Generally requires 12th with PCM and min percentage (often 45-50%). Domicile rules apply. Check specific state's CET website.

  • BITSAT, VITEEE, SRMJEEE, MET (Manipal), etc. (Private/Deemed University Exams)
Career Prospects: Software, Civil, Mechanical, Electrical, Electronics, Computer Science, Aerospace, Chemical Engg., etc.
Architecture (B.Arch) +
Key Exams:
  • NATA (National Aptitude Test in Architecture) +

    • Eligibility: Passed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry & Maths OR 10+3 Diploma with Maths. Min 50% aggregate in PCM and 50% overall in 10+2 / Diploma.

    Official NATA Website

  • JEE Main (Paper 2A - B.Arch)
Career Prospects: Architect, Urban Planner, Interior Designer, Landscape Architect.
B.Sc. (Physics, Chemistry, Maths, CS, IT, Stats, etc.) +
Key Exams:
  • CUET-UG (Common University Entrance Test) +

    • Eligibility: Passed 12th. Specific subject combinations required for different B.Sc courses vary by university. Check eligibility mapping on CUET/University website.

    Official CUET-UG Website

  • State University Entrances / Merit-based admission
  • NEST (Integrated M.Sc. at NISER/CEBS)
  • IAT (IISER Aptitude Test for BS-MS)
Career Prospects: Scientist, Researcher, Teacher/Professor (after higher studies), Data Analyst, Statistician, IT Professional, Lab Technician.
BCA (Bachelor of Computer Applications) +
Key Exams:
  • CUET-UG (For Central Universities)
  • University Specific Entrance Tests (e.g., IPU CET)
  • Merit-based admission in many colleges

Eligibility (General): Passed 10+2. Many universities require Maths/Computer Science as a subject in 12th. Check specific institution criteria.

Career Prospects: Software Developer, Web Developer, System Analyst, Network Administrator, Database Manager (often requires further specialization or MCA).
Defence (via NDA - National Defence Academy) +
Key Exam:
  • UPSC NDA & NA Exam +

    • Eligibility (Army Wing): 12th Pass (any stream). (Air Force/Naval Wings): 12th Pass with Physics, Chemistry & Maths. Strict age limits (approx 16.5-19.5 years, unmarried male/female). Physical standards apply.

    UPSC Official Website

Career Prospects: Officer in Indian Army, Navy, or Air Force.
B.Des (Design), Integrated M.Sc., Merchant Navy courses, etc.
Science (PCB - Physics, Chemistry, Biology) +
Medical (MBBS, BDS, AYUSH) +
Key Exam:
  • NEET-UG (National Eligibility cum Entrance Test) +

    • Eligibility: Passed 12th with Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Biotechnology & English individually. Minimum 50% aggregate in PCB (40% for SC/ST/OBC-NCL, 45% for PwD). Age: Min 17 years as of Dec 31 of admission year.

    Official NEET-UG Website (NTA)

Courses Covered by NEET: MBBS, BDS, BAMS (Ayurveda), BHMS (Homeopathy), BUMS (Unani), BSMS (Siddha), B.V.Sc & AH (Veterinary - some seats). Career Prospects: Doctor (various specializations), Dentist, AYUSH Practitioner.
Veterinary Science (B.V.Sc & AH) +
Key Exams:
  • NEET-UG (For 15% All India Quota & many state seats)
  • State Level Entrances (Some states may have separate exams)
  • ICAR AIEEA (Limited seats in some universities)

Eligibility (General): Similar to NEET eligibility (12th PCB + English, min percentage). Check VCI/State norms.

Career Prospects: Veterinarian (Govt./Private/Own Clinic), Researcher, Animal Husbandry Officer.
Pharmacy (B.Pharm) +
Key Exams:
  • State CETs (e.g., MHT-CET Pharmacy, WBJEE Pharmacy)
  • CUET-UG (For some central/participating universities)
  • University Specific Exams (e.g., BITSAT Pharmacy)

Eligibility (General): 10+2 with PCB or PCM. Min percentage required varies. Check Pharmacy Council of India (PCI) & specific college norms.

Career Prospects: Pharmacist (Retail/Hospital), Production/QA/QC in Pharma Industry, Medical Representative, Drug Inspector (Govt.).
Nursing (B.Sc Nursing) +
Key Exams:
  • AIIMS Nursing Entrance
  • JIPMER Nursing Entrance
  • NEET-UG (Some institutions may use NEET scores - check specific college)
  • State Level Nursing Entrances / University Exams

Eligibility (General): 10+2 with PCB & English. Minimum percentage required (often 45-50%). Check Indian Nursing Council (INC) & specific college norms.

Career Prospects: Staff Nurse, Nursing Supervisor, Educator, Administrator in Hospitals/Clinics/Community Health.
Allied Health / Paramedical (BPT, BMLT, B.Optom, etc.) +
Courses: BPT (Physiotherapy), BMLT (Medical Lab Tech), B.Sc Radiology/Imaging Tech, B.Optom (Optometry), B.Sc OT Technology, B.Sc Dialysis Tech, etc. Key Exams:
  • University Specific Entrances
  • State Level Paramedical Entrances
  • Merit-based admissions are common

Eligibility (General): 10+2 with PCB. Minimum percentage varies. Check specific course/college norms.

Career Prospects: Physiotherapist, Lab Technician, Radiographer, Optometrist, Operation Theatre Technician, Dialysis Technician.
B.Sc. (Botany, Zoology, Microbio, Biotech, Life Sci, etc.) +
Key Exams:
  • CUET-UG (Common University Entrance Test) +

    • Eligibility: Passed 12th with PCB or relevant science subjects. Specific combinations required vary by course/university. Check CUET/University website.

    Official CUET-UG Website

  • State University Entrances / Merit-based admission
  • ICAR AIEEA (For B.Sc Agriculture, Horticulture, Fisheries, etc.)
  • NEST (Integrated M.Sc. at NISER/CEBS)
  • IAT (IISER Aptitude Test for BS-MS)
Career Prospects: Researcher, Scientist, Teacher/Professor (after higher studies), Lab Technician, Biotechnologist, Microbiologist, Agriculturist, Horticulturist, Environmental Scientist.
Commerce +
B.Com (Bachelor of Commerce - Gen/Hons) +
Key Exams / Admission:
  • CUET-UG (For Central/Participating Universities)
  • Merit-based (based on 12th marks) in many State Universities/Colleges
  • University Specific Entrance Tests (e.g., NPAT for NMIMS)

Eligibility (General): 10+2 Commerce. Some universities might prefer/require Maths for Hons. Check specific university criteria.

Career Prospects: Accountant, Tax Consultant, Auditor (often needs CA), Banking, Financial Services, Insurance, Govt Jobs (e.g., SSC CGL Auditor/Accountant). Foundation for CA/CS/CMA, MBA.
BBA / BMS (Management) +
Key Exams:
  • CUET-UG
  • IPMAT (For 5-year Integrated MBA at IIM Indore/Rohtak/etc.)
  • NPAT (NMIMS), SET (Symbiosis), DU JAT (If conducted), Christ University ET
  • University Specific Entrances / Merit-based

Eligibility (General): 10+2 (Any stream, often Commerce preferred). Many top colleges require/prefer Maths. Check specific institution criteria.

Career Prospects: Entry-level Management roles, Marketing, Sales, HR, Operations, Banking. Good foundation for MBA.
Professional Courses (CA / CS / CMA) +
Courses & Institutes:
  • CA (Chartered Accountancy) - Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI)
  • CS (Company Secretary) - Institute of Company Secretaries of India (ICSI)
  • CMA (Cost & Management Accountancy) - Institute of Cost Accountants of India (ICMAI)
Admission Process: Register for Foundation course after 10+2 (any stream). Clear Foundation -> Intermediate -> Final exams along with practical training.

Exams: Foundation Exams conducted by respective institutes.

ICAI | ICSI | ICMAI

Career Prospects: Chartered Accountant, Company Secretary, Cost Accountant in industry or practice. High demand roles.
B.A. / B.Sc. Economics (Hons) +
Key Exams:
  • CUET-UG (Most prominent route now)
  • University Specific Entrances (e.g., Christ University ET)

Eligibility (General): 10+2. Strong preference / requirement for Mathematics in 12th by most top universities. Check specific university criteria.

Career Prospects: Economist, Policy Analyst, Data Analyst, Financial Analyst, Consultant (often requires Master's degree). Foundation for Indian Economic Service (IES), MBA.
Law (Integrated 5-Year B.Com LLB / BBA LLB) +
Key Exams:
  • CLAT (Common Law Admission Test) +

    • Eligibility: 10+2 (Any stream) with min 45% marks (40% for SC/ST). No upper age limit.

    CLAT Consortium

  • AILET (NLU Delhi), LSAT-India (Private Univ.), SET (Symbiosis Law), State Law CETs
Career Prospects: Lawyer (Corporate, Litigation, etc.), Judge (after experience/Judicial Services Exam), Legal Advisor, Law Professor.
B.Des (Design), BCA (if Maths), Hotel Management, etc.
Arts / Humanities +
B.A. (Bachelor of Arts - Various Subjects) +
Subjects: History, Political Science, Sociology, Psychology, English Lit., Geography, Philosophy, Languages, etc. Key Exams / Admission:
  • CUET-UG (Major route for Central/Participating Universities)
  • Merit-based (12th marks) in many State Universities/Colleges
  • University Specific Entrance Tests

Eligibility (General): 10+2 (Any stream, usually Arts preferred). Specific subject requirements might exist for Hons courses in some universities.

Career Prospects: Teacher/Professor (needs higher studies), Content Writer, Editor, Journalist, Archaeologist, Historian, Political Analyst, Psychologist (needs Master's), Social Worker, Civil Services, Govt Jobs.
Law (Integrated 5-Year B.A. LLB) +
Key Exams:
  • CLAT (Common Law Admission Test) +

    • Eligibility: 10+2 (Any stream) with min 45% marks (40% for SC/ST). No upper age limit.

    CLAT Consortium

  • AILET (NLU Delhi), LSAT-India (Private Univ.), SET (Symbiosis Law), State Law CETs
Career Prospects: Lawyer (Corporate, Litigation, Human Rights, etc.), Judge, Legal Advisor, Policy Analyst.
Journalism & Mass Communication (BJMC / BA JMC) +
Key Exams / Admission:
  • CUET-UG (For some universities)
  • University Specific Entrance Tests (e.g., IPU CET, Symbiosis SET, JMI Entrance)
  • Merit-based in some institutions

Eligibility (General): 10+2 (Any stream).

Career Prospects: Journalist, Reporter, Editor, Content Writer, Public Relations Officer, Advertising Executive, Radio Jockey, TV Anchor.
Hotel Management (BHM / B.Sc HM) +
Key Exams:
  • NCHM JEE (National Council for Hotel Management) - For IHMs
  • University/College Specific Entrances (e.g., WGSHA Manipal, Christ University ET)

Eligibility (General): 10+2 (Any stream) with English as a compulsory subject. Age limits may apply. Check NCHM/College norms.

Career Prospects: Managerial roles in Hotels, Restaurants, Airlines, Cruise Lines, Catering Companies, Event Management.
Design (B.Des / BFA) +
Fields: Fashion, Textile, Graphic, Product, Interior Design, Fine Arts (Painting, Sculpture, etc.) Key Exams:
  • UCEED (IIT Bombay - for B.Des in IITs)
  • NID DAT (National Institute of Design)
  • NIFT Entrance Exam (National Institutes of Fashion Technology)
  • CUET-UG (For BFA/Design in some universities)
  • College Specific Aptitude Tests / Portfolio Reviews

Eligibility (General): 10+2 (Any stream usually). Specific requirements & portfolio needs vary.

Career Prospects: Fashion Designer, Graphic Designer, Animator, Product Designer, Interior Designer, Artist, Art Director.
Social Work (BSW), Elementary Education (B.El.Ed), Event Management Courses, Travel & Tourism Courses, etc.
