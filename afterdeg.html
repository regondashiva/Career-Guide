Career Guidance: After Bachelor's Degree

Explore higher study options and entrance exams in India.

Choose Your Graduation Background:

Completed Bachelor's Degree
B.E. / B.Tech +
M.Tech / M.E. +
Exams:
  • GATE (Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering) +

    • Eligibility (General): Completed B.E./B.Tech or in final year. Specific discipline requirements apply. No age limit. Valid score for 3 years for admissions/PSUs.

    Official GATE Website (Example - Check Current Year's Site)

  • State Level PGCETs (e.g., Karnataka PGCET, TS PGECET) +

    • Eligibility (General): Varies by state. Usually requires B.E./B.Tech from a recognized university within the state or nationally. Check specific state's PGCET notification.

  • Specific Institute Exams (e.g., IIIT Hyderabad PGEE)
MBA (Master of Business Administration) +
Exams:
  • CAT (Common Admission Test) +

    • Eligibility (General): Bachelor's Degree (any discipline, including B.Tech) with min 50% marks (45% for SC/ST/PwD). Final year students can apply.

    Official CAT Website

  • XAT (Xavier Aptitude Test) +

    • Eligibility (General): Bachelor's Degree (any discipline). Final year students can apply. No percentage bar specified by XLRI.

    Official XAT Website

  • CMAT, MAT, ATMA, GMAT, State MBA CETs
M.S. (Master of Science) - Abroad +
Requirements (General):
  • Relevant B.Tech degree with good GPA.
  • GRE (Graduate Record Examinations).
  • TOEFL/IELTS (English Proficiency).
  • Statement of Purpose (SOP), Letters of Recommendation (LORs).
  • Varies significantly by country and university.
Govt. Jobs / PSUs +
Routes:
  • UPSC ESE (Engineering Services Examination) - For specific core engineering branches.
  • PSU Recruitment through GATE scores (e.g., IOCL, NTPC, ONGC).
  • UPSC CSE (Civil Services Exam) - Open to all graduates.
  • SSC JE (Junior Engineer) / Other technical posts.
  • Banking Sector (IBPS/SBI Specialist Officers - IT Officer).

Eligibility: Varies greatly based on the specific job/exam. Check official notifications.

Research / Ph.D. (often requires Master's first)
Other Degree (B.Sc., B.A., B.Com, etc.) +
Master's Degree (M.Sc./M.A./M.Com) +
Exams:
  • CUET-PG (Common University Entrance Test - PG) +

    • Eligibility (General): Relevant Bachelor's Degree. Specific subject requirements and minimum percentages vary by university and course. Check specific university criteria via CUET-PG portal.

    Official CUET-PG Website

  • IIT JAM (For M.Sc. in IITs/IISc) +

    • Eligibility (General): Bachelor's Degree with the required subjects (e.g., Physics/Maths/Chem/Bio) and minimum 55% (50% for SC/ST/PwD). Final year students eligible.

    Official JAM Website (Example - Check Current Year's Site)

  • TISSNET (Tata Institute of Social Sciences - MA programs)
  • Specific University Entrance Exams
MBA (Master of Business Administration) +
Exams:
  • CAT (Common Admission Test) +

    • Eligibility (General): Bachelor's Degree (any discipline) with min 50% marks (45% for SC/ST/PwD). Final year students can apply.

    Official CAT Website

  • XAT (Xavier Aptitude Test) +

    • Eligibility (General): Bachelor's Degree (any discipline). Final year students can apply. No percentage bar specified by XLRI.

    Official XAT Website

  • CMAT, MAT, ATMA, GMAT, State MBA CETs
MCA (Master of Computer Applications) +
Exams:
  • NIMCET (NIT MCA Common Entrance Test) +

    • Eligibility (General): B.Sc./BCA/B.Sc.(Hons)/BIT with Mathematics/Statistics as one subject OR B.E./B.Tech. Minimum 60% marks (55% for SC/ST). Final year eligible.

    Official NIMCET Website

  • CUET-PG (For MCA in participating universities)
  • State Level MCA CETs
Law (3-Year LL.B.) +
Exams:
  • State Level Law CETs (e.g., MAH CET Law, TS LAWCET)
  • DU LLB Entrance Exam
  • LSAT India (Accepted by some private universities)
  • BHU PET (For Law)

Eligibility (General): Any Bachelor's Degree with minimum percentage (varies, often 45-50%).

B.Ed. (Bachelor of Education) +
Exams:
  • State Level B.Ed. CETs (e.g., UP B.Ed JEE, TS EdCET)
  • CUET-PG (For B.Ed. in participating universities)
  • University Specific Exams

Eligibility (General): Bachelor's or Master's Degree with minimum 50-55% marks. Subject requirements may apply based on teaching specialization.

Govt. Jobs +
Exams:
  • UPSC CSE (Civil Services Exam)
  • SSC CGL (Combined Graduate Level)
  • IBPS PO / Clerk (Banking)
  • SBI PO / Clerk (Banking)
  • RBI Grade B / Assistant
  • State PSC Exams
  • Railway Recruitment Boards (RRB) Exams

Eligibility (General): Usually any Bachelor's Degree. Age limits and specific requirements vary. Check official notifications.

Note: Eligibility criteria are general guidelines. Always refer to the official websites of the exams and institutions for the latest, most accurate information, including specific subject requirements, percentage criteria, age limits, and reservation policies. Deadlines are crucial!
